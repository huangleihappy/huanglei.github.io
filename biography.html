Xiaoyu Wang – Biography

Xiaoyu Wang received the B.S. degree in Mathematics from
Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2015, and the Ph.D. degree
in Computational Mathematics from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences
Academy of Mathematics and System Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences
, Beijing, China, in 2020. She has been a postdoctoral researcher at the division
of decision and control, school of electrical engineering and computer science, KTH,
working with Prof. Mikael Johansson. Her research
interests include stochastic optimization and large-scale optimization algorithms.
