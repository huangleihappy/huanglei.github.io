Short Bio

I am a SEW Visiting Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at UCSD. My mentor is Prof. Jiawang Nie. I completed my Ph.D. degree in computational mathematics from Academy of Mathematics and System Science, Chinese Academy of Sciences, under the supervision of Prof. Ya-Xiang Yuan, in July 2023, and I got my B.S. degree in mathematics from School of Mathematics and Statistics, Wuhan University in July 2018.

My research interest includes:

  • Polynomial Optimization

  • Semidefinite Optimization

  • Matrix and Tensor Computation
